Concrete
Strength.Predictor

Concrete's compressive strength doesn't come from a formula you can do in your head — cement, water, and curing age interact rather than simply adding up. This project trains two models on 1,030 real lab-tested mixes to predict strength (MPa) from mix design, then compares a simple baseline against one that can actually capture that interaction.

03 — Interpretation

What Drives the Prediction

How much the Random Forest actually leans on each input to make its guess. Age and cement are nearly tied at the top, together accounting for about two-thirds of the model's decisions.

04 — Direction

Which Way Each Ingredient Pushes

Importance says how much an input matters; this says which direction. Cement and superplasticizer push strength up — water pulls it down, the classic water-cement ratio effect.
